    Our Services

  • Psychiatry

    We provide expert mental health care, offering diagnosis, treatment, and support for a wide range of psychiatric conditions. Our compassionate team is here to help you achieve emotional well-being.

  • Gynecology

    Comprehensive women’s health services, including preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment for reproductive health issues. Our specialists are dedicated to supporting women at every stage of life.

  • Pulmonology

    Specialized care for lung and respiratory conditions, including asthma, COPD, pneumonia, and other breathing disorders. Our experts provide accurate diagnosis and advanced treatment to help breathe easier.

  • Orthopedics

    Comprehensive care for bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments. Our specialists provide expert diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation for injuries and musculoskeletal conditions to help restore mobility and strength.


  • Cardiology

    Specialized care for heart and cardiovascular conditions, including hypertension, arrhythmia, heart disease, and heart failure. Our cardiologists provide precise diagnosis and advanced treatments to keep your heart healthy.

  • Pediatrics

    Comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents, covering growth, development, immunizations, and common childhood illnesses. Our pediatricians ensure your child’s health and well-being at every stage.

  • Osteology

    Focused care on the study and treatment of bones, including bone diseases like osteoporosis, fractures, and skeletal health management to maintain strong and healthy bones.



  • Neurology

    Specialized care for disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. We diagnose and treat conditions like epilepsy, stroke, migraines, and neuropathies.

  • Urology

    Expert care for urinary tract and male reproductive system conditions, including kidney stones, infections, and prostate health.


  • Ophthalmology

    Specialized care for eye health, vision correction, and treatment of eye diseases like glaucoma, cataracts, and retinal disorders.



  • Dermatology

    Comprehensive care for skin, hair, and nail conditions, including acne, eczema, and skin cancer screening.



  • Oncology

    Comprehensive cancer care offering early detection, precise diagnosis, and personalized treatment plans including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgery, all supported by compassionate patient-centered care.


  • Gastroenterology

    Specialized care for digestive system conditions, including ulcers, liver disease, irritable bowel syndrome, and gastrointestinal cancers. Our team offers advanced diagnosis and treatment for optimal digestive health.


  • Radiology

    Advanced imaging services including X-rays, CT scans, MRI, and ultrasound to aid accurate diagnosis and treatment planning. Our radiologists collaborate closely with other specialists to ensure timely, precise results for optimal patient care.


  • Medicine, ICU Care & 24/7 Superfast Ambulance Service

    Comprehensive medical care supported by advanced ICU facilities and fully equipped operation theaters. Our 24/7 superfast ambulance service ensures prompt emergency response and transport.
